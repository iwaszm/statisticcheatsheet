Key Assumptions Dictionary

Understanding the prerequisites for your statistical models is crucial to avoid false positives/negatives.

Independence (独立性)

Data points do not influence each other. A person's score does not affect another's.

Normality of residuals (残差正态性)

The difference between observed and predicted values should be normally distributed.

Homogeneity of variance (方差齐性)

Also called homoscedasticity. The variance within each group should be roughly equal.

Sphericity (球形假设)

Only for repeated measures (rmANOVA). Differences variances are equal.

Multicollinearity (多重共线性)

Predictors should not be too highly correlated. Checked via VIF.
